Privacy Policy for StepFlow

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

Overview

StepFlow ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the StepFlow mobile application.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. StepFlow is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • All gait analysis and processing happens locally on your device
  • Health data from Apple Watch stays on your device
  • We never access, store, or share your health information
  • No user accounts or login credentials are required

HealthKit Data

StepFlow uses Apple's HealthKit framework to access motion and fitness data from your Apple Watch and iPhone. This data includes:

  • Walking speed and cadence
  • Step count and stride length
  • Motion sensor data (accelerometer, gyroscope)

All HealthKit data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers. You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time through your iPhone's Settings.

AI Processing

Our AI-powered gait analysis runs entirely on your device using Apple's Core ML framework. No data is sent to external servers for processing.

Third-Party Services

StepFlow does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Subscription Information

Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs how your purchase information is handled.

Children's Privacy

StepFlow is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
